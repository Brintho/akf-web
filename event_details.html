Mindful Breathing Workshop

December 28, 2024 10:00 AM - 12:00 PM Online via Zoom

About This Workshop

In our fast-paced world, the simple act of breathing is often overlooked. This practical workshop is designed to help you reconnect with your breath, transforming it into a powerful tool for stress reduction, mental clarity, and overall well-being. Whether you are a beginner or have some experience with meditation, this session will provide valuable techniques to integrate into your daily life.

What You Will Learn:

  • The science behind mindful breathing and its effect on the nervous system.
  • Diaphragmatic (Belly) Breathing for deep relaxation.
  • The 4-7-8 technique to calm anxiety and aid sleep.
  • How to use your breath as an anchor during meditation.
  • Simple exercises to practice anytime, anywhere.

Hosted by Ananya Sharma

Certified Mindfulness Coach

Ananya has over 15 years of experience in yogic sciences and has guided thousands of individuals on their path to inner peace.
