About Us

A Sanctuary for Timeless Wisdom and Modern Spirituality

Goals and objectives

Our goal

Our main goal is to share timeless spiritual knowledge in a modern context. We are committed to creating a supportive and inclusive environment for personal growth, mindful living, and deep self-discovery. We believe that the combination of ancient wisdom and modern science helps to build a balanced and meaningful life.

Our Mission

Our mission is to create a global community where everyone is encouraged to follow their spiritual path. We help people find self-awareness, compassion, and inner peace through various courses, workshops, and discussions. Our activities are open to all, so that the light of knowledge and harmony can spread everywhere.

History of the organization

2010

The beginning of a dream

Our journey began as a small study group with a few like-minded individuals, whose purpose was to discuss the knowledge of the Vedas and Upanishads.

2014

Formal Establishment

Due to growing interest, we officially launched as a non-profit organization called 'Arya Kalyan Foundation' and set up our first community center.

2018

Digital Footprint

To reach people worldwide, we launched our website and online course platform, making our knowledge base accessible to all.

2023

International Recognition

We have earned international recognition for our educational activities and social contributions and have become a strong family of 10,000+ members around the world.
