Sindoor Khela & Cultural Celebration

24 October 2023 | Kumartuli, Kolkata

Date & Time
24 Oct 2023, 3:00 PM
Location
Kumartuli, Kolkata
Category
Cultural

A vibrant celebration marking the culmination of Durga Puja. Join us for the traditional Sindoor Khela, a joyous ritual where married women apply vermilion to each other, followed by an evening of cultural performances.
